Dad Tribute

My dad is the best role model I could ever have. Through his hard work and dedication, he has provided me and our family with a comfortable life—something I will always appreciate. If not for him, I wouldn’t be the person I am today. This tribute is for my amazing dad, Mike Legaspi.


Here is a timeline of my dad's life and achievements:

  • 1982 – Born in Bocaue, Bulacan
  • 2004 – Graduated college with a Bachelor's Degree in Political Science in Adamson University
  • 2005 – Marries Shella Nebre
  • 2011 – Goes abroad and starts working at Sichem
  • 2012 – Starts being a drummer for the Filipino Christian Church
  • 2016 – Gets promoted as the manager of Sichem's Logistics Department
  • 2021 – Goes back to the Philippines to start his own business Sabon Fabrika
    • “All for the glory of God.”
